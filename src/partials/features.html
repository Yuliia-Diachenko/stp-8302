Main Gameplay Features

💚Triple Tile Matching - Match three identical tiles to clear the board and progress through levels.

🌱 Relaxing Atmosphere - Enjoy calming visuals and soothing background music.

🔄 Challenging Levels - Each level introduces new puzzles and unique challenges.

🎉 Daily Rewards & Events - Unlock exciting prizes and participate in time-limited challenges.

🔎 Boosters & Power-ups - Use helpful tools to solve tricky puzzles with ease.

🛠️ Customizable Themes - Choose from beautiful themes to personalize your experience.

🏆 Leaderboard & Achievements - Track your progress and compete with friends.

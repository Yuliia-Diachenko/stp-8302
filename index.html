About the Game

Peace Match: Triple Tile takes the classic tile-matching puzzle to new heights with a calming and visually stunning experience. Immerse yourself in a world of soothing landscapes and let your mind relax as you strategically match three identical tiles to clear the board. With a blend of traditional puzzle mechanics and modern design elements, Peace Match offers an engaging and stress-free gaming journey where every match brings a sense of achievement and tranquility.

How to Play

Tap & Match - Find and select three identical tiles to remove them from the board.

Strategize Your Moves - Plan your matches carefully to clear levels efficiently.

Use Boosters - Stuck? Use power-ups like shuffles and hints to continue playing.

Advance Through Levels - Conquer challenging puzzles and unlock new stages.

Compete & Achieve - Track your progress, earn stars, and climb leaderboards.

Enjoy Daily Challenges - Complete special missions for exclusive rewards.
